Here is the story of a man named Charlie. On a tragic and fateful day, he put ten cents in his pocket, kissed his wife and family and went to ride on the MTA.

Did he ever return? No! He never returned and his fate is still unlearned. Observe his know path and suggest additional trains that Charlie might have taken. Otherwise, he may ride forever ‘neath the streets of Boston and be the man who never returned.
